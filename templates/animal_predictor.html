An AI predictor designed to identify endangered animals based on physical features operates by utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms and a comprehensive dataset of animal characteristics.

 

When a user provides the details , the AI processes this information through a trained model. This model has been developed using a vast collection of labeled data, where each entry corresponds to a specific animal and its features.

 

By analyzing the input features, the AI compares them against its database to find the closest match. The system then predicts the name of the endangered animal that best fits the provided description. This technology not only aids in quick and accurate identification but also supports conservation efforts by raising awareness and facilitating research on endangered species.
